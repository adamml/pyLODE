Biodiversity 1ni

Classes(s)
http://something/national-marine-planning-framework-policies#SecondaryPolicy
URI http://something/national-marine-planning-framework-policies#2
Description Proposals incorporating features that enhance or facilitate species adaptation or migration, or natural native habitat connectivity will be supported, subject to the outcome of statutory environmental assessment processes and subsequent decision by the competent authority, and where they contribute to the policies and objectives of this NMPF. Proposals that may have significant adverse impacts on species adaptation or migration, or on natural native habitat connectivity must demonstrate that they will, in order of preference and in accordance with legal requirements:
  1. avoid,
  2. minimise, or
  3. mitigate
significant adverse impacts on species adaptation or migration, or on natural native habitat connectivity.
Applies to Whole Maritime Area true

Biodiversity 2ni

Classes(s)
http://something/national-marine-planning-framework-policies#SecondaryPolicy
URI http://something/national-marine-planning-framework-policies#3
Description Proposals that protect, maintain, restore and enhance the distribution and net extent of important habitats and distribution of important species will be supported, subject to the outcome of statutory environmental assessment processes and subsequent decision by the competent authority, and where they contribute to the policies and objectives of this NMPF. Proposals must avoid significant reduction in the distribution and net extent of important habitats and other habitats that important species depend on, including avoidance of activity that may result in disturbance or displacement of habitats
Applies to Whole Maritime Area true

Biodiversity 3ni

Classes(s)
http://something/national-marine-planning-framework-policies#Policy
URI http://something/national-marine-planning-framework-policies#4
Description Where marine or coastal natural capital assets are recognised by Government:
  • Proposals must seek to enhance marine or coastal natural capital assets where possible.
  • Proposals must demonstrate that they will in order of preference, and in accordance with legal requirements:
  1. avoid,
  2. minimise, or
  3. mitigate
  4. if it is not possible to mitigate significant adverse impacts on marine or coastal natural capital assets proposals must set out the reason for proceeding.
Applies to Whole Maritime Area true
